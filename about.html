About

ReSource Materials is building advanced technology for critical materials recovery

The company is focused on practical innovation at the intersection of magnetics, materials, transport, and separation science.

Mission

ReSource Materials exists to develop advanced separation technologies that improve recovery of valuable materials from complex and underutilized feedstocks.

Vision

Our vision is to contribute to stronger domestic supply chains, more sustainable resource use, and practical processing solutions for critical materials.

What Makes the Company Different

ReSource Materials is grounded in deep technical expertise spanning magnetism, transport phenomena, materials physics, and process development. This combination enables a distinctive approach to difficult separation problems.

Collaboration

ReSource Materials is interested in collaboration with research partners, industrial stakeholders, and organizations working in critical minerals, recycling, advanced materials, and related areas.

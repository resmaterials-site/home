What We Do

Technology Development

Applied Materials Recovery

Our target applications include battery recycling, rare earth recovery, and processing of heterogeneous electronic waste and industrial byproducts.

Scalable Solutions

ReSource Materials is focused on scalable process concepts that can bridge laboratory innovation with pilot-scale implementation and commercialization.

Applications

Where our technology can create impact

Battery Recycling

Recovery of critical elements from black mass and other battery-recycling streams using selective separation strategies designed for complex particle mixtures.

Rare Earth Recovery

Separation and concentration of rare earth and other strategic species from mixed powders, wastes, and industrial residues.

Electronic Waste

New process pathways for recovering valuable materials from shredded printed circuit boards and other heterogeneous e-waste streams.

Applications

Applications in critical materials recovery and advanced resource processing

ReSource Materials is focused on separation challenges where improved selectivity can unlock better recovery, better economics, and more sustainable use of valuable materials.

Battery Recycling

Battery recycling streams often contain complex mixtures of active materials, metallic particles, carbonaceous materials, and other components. Advanced separation approaches may improve recovery of valuable constituents from black mass and related feedstocks.

Rare Earth Recovery

Rare earth supply chains are strategically important and often constrained by difficult processing and separation steps. ReSource Materials is interested in technologies that can support more selective recovery and concentration of rare earth-bearing materials.

Electronic Waste Processing

Electronic waste is highly heterogeneous and contains valuable metals and metal compounds embedded in challenging mixtures. New separation strategies can help unlock resource value while reducing waste.

Industrial Byproducts and Complex Feedstocks

Many industrial residues contain useful materials that remain underutilized because they are difficult to separate. Magnetic and hybrid separation methods may create new pathways for resource recovery.

Built for practical impact

The company is interested in applications where advanced physics-based separation can support domestic supply chains, critical-materials resilience, and more efficient use of resources.
